About Pthree3 Handmade Jewelry

Do not let truth and mercy leave you; bind them around your neck, write them on the tablet of your heart. Proverbs 3:3

Pthree3 was created by a Christ-following wife and mother. The beauty and truth in each of God's names inspired her to create jewelry showcasing His nature. The Lord is both truth and mercy, and Pthree3 has taken the directive given in Proverbs 3:3 literally, to help you bind the Lord's name around your neck.

May the Lord write Himself on the tablet of your heart, beloved.

Placing an Order

Welcome to Pthree3 Handmade Jewelry! We are thrilled that you are interested in our artisan-made jewelry. Placing an order is simple. Just browse through our collection, select the items you love, and proceed to checkout. We accept various secure payment options and offer free shipping to ensure your order reaches you promptly and with no hidden costs.

Custom Orders

If you are seeking a specific name of God on a necklace not currently available in our shop, please contact us @Pthree3Jewelry@gmail.com to request your custom jewelry. We are happy to work with you to create a necklace that you love!

Return/Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing our company. We take pride in the quality of our products and services. Due to the handcrafted, customized nature of our business, we do not accept returns or refunds. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in this matter. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us.
